About us

Who We Are

Unifocus is a niche branding and marketing firm helping brands leverage the power of creativity, experience and expertise to effectively use the power of digital to increase brand awareness, convert more leads and optimize user experience of products and services. Having worked with over a 100 startups and multinationals, our team has a strong understanding of the online ecosystem and strategies that work and the ones that generally don't in generating higher conversions and building a strong brand.

We work along with our clients to better understand their desired objectives and create tailor-made solutions to cater to their specific business requirements. Our process starts with Strategy and Planning where we work along with our clients to better understand the product /service and the target segment. Once a strong brand is created, we find out innovative ways of reaching out the intended target segment using various online(and sometimes offline) channels.
Our solutions have been bucketed into three categories as briefly illustrated below.

Brand strategy, design and development

We understand the impact a rightly positioned brand can have to businesses, which is why we we put in meticulous time and effort is to create lasting brand identities. Right brand positioning can have a big impact on how your service or product is perceived in the market. To understand your competitors, find the right market segment and understand your customer's persona is vital for the success of a business. We use a mix of creativity and analytics to find the right markets, competitors, unique value propositions based on which we position and create your brand's unique identity.
